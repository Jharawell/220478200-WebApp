NCT Dream

NCT DREAM is NCT's sub-group comprising seven members – MARK, RENJUN, JENO, HAECHAN, JAEMIN, CHENLE and JISUNG. The once “teenage team” now grew up as one of the iconic K-POP boy bands.

Wayv

WayV is a six-member boy group formed by SM Entertainment, and the fourth and the Chinese sub-unit of NCT. Originally as seven, they debuted in China on January 17, 2019 with their first digital EP, The Vision.

NCT Wish

NCT Wish is a Japanese boy band and the final sub-unit of the South Korean boy band NCT, formed and managed by SM Entertainment and Avex Trax with SM director and soloist BoA, in charge as the producer.
